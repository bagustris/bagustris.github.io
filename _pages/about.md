> Speech AI researcher and educator at NAIST. I build open-source tools,
> tutorials, and demos in speech processing — from speech classification (e.g., emotion recognition) to
> ASR and TTS.

## What I work on

### 🔬 Research
Speech emotion recognition, multimodal fusion, multitask learning, and
pathological / health-related voice analysis. See
[research themes](/research/) and the
[full publication list](/publications/).

### 🛠️ Tools
Open-source toolkits I build and maintain:

- [**Nkululeko**](https://nkululeko.readthedocs.io) — high-level framework for predicting speaker characteristics from speech.
- [**Speechain**](https://bagustris.github.io/speechain) — utilities for speech processing and analysis.
- [**PaperRAG**](https://bagustris.github.io/paperrag) — local-first, CPU-friendly RAG over research papers.

More on the [Tools](/tools/) page or my [GitHub profile](https://github.com/bagustris).

### 📚 Tutorials
Hands-on tutorials on shell, Python, signal processing, NLP, speech recognition,
Git, and LaTeX. Browse the [tutorials index](/tutorials/).

## About

**Do! Don't just read!** — Bagus Tris Atmaja is a researcher and lecturer in
speech and audio processing. He received the B.E. and M.E. degrees from the
Sepuluh Nopember Institute of Technology in 2009 and 2012, respectively, and
the Ph.D. degree in information science with a focus on speech emotion
recognition from the Japan Advanced Institute of Science and Technology in 2021. 
He was a Docent with the Vibrastic Laboratory, Sepuluh Nopember
Institute of Technology since 2014, and a Researcher with the Artificial
Intelligence Research Center, National Institute of Advanced Industrial
Science and Technology (AIST). He is currently an Assistant Professor at NAIST.
His research interests include speech processing, multimodal fusion, and
multitask learning.

## **Do! Don't just read!**

BAGUS TRIS ATMAJA is a researcher and lecturer in speech and audio processing. He received the B.E. and M.E. degrees from the Sepuluh Nopember Institute of Technology, in 2009 and 2012, respectively, and the Ph.D. degree in information science with a focus on speech emotion recognition from the Japan Advanced Institute of Science and Technology, in 2021. He was employed as a Docent with the Vibrastic Laboratory, Sepuluh Nopember Institute of Technology since 2014. He was also a Postdoctoral and currently a Researcher with the Artificial Intelligence Research Center, National Institute of Advanced Industrial Science and Technology (AIST). His research interests include speech processing, affective computing, multimodal recognition, and multitask learning.
